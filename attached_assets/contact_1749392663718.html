How can I track my order?

Once your order is processed and shipped, you'll receive an email with your tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also log in to your account on our website to view your order status and tracking information.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 15 days of delivery for unopened products in their original packaging. For quality concerns, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order. We'll provide a return label and process your refund once we receive the returned items.

Do you offer wholesale prices?

Yes, we offer special wholesale pricing for restaurants, retailers, and bulk purchasers. Our wholesale program includes discounts based on order volume and flexible payment terms. Contact our sales team at wholesale@delispi.com for more information and to set up a wholesale account.

Are your spices organic?

Many of our spices are certified organic, and we clearly label them as such on our website and packaging. We also offer conventional options. All our products, whether organic or conventional, meet our strict quality standards for purity and flavor.

How should I store my spices?

For maximum freshness, store spices in airtight containers in a cool, dark place away from heat and moisture. Whole spices maintain their flavor for about 4 years, ground spices for 2-3 years. Our packaging is designed to preserve freshness, but transferring to glass containers after opening can extend shelf life.
